We are a local high school media creator based in Fishers, Indiana, with four years of hands-on experience in photography and videography — especially in the world of sports. What began as a fun hobby quickly grew into a passion, and now, a serious career focus.

Over the years, we’ve had the chance to stand on the sidelines of major sporting events and work face-to-face with some of the top high school athletes in the region. From high school gyms to prep school showcases, we’ve developed a fast, energetic style that captures the heart of the game.
